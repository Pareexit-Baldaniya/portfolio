Projects

Fingerprint Door Unlock System

A fingerprint door unlocking system is a secure access control method that uses a person's unique fingerprint as a key. It involves capturing a fingerprint, comparing it against a stored database, and authorizing or denying access based on the match. This technology offers a convenient and highly secure way to control access to doors, buildings, or other restricted areas.

Face Detection System

Face Detection System is a computer vision technique that identifies and locates human faces within an image or video frame. It involves training a machine learning model on a large dataset of labeled images to recognize facial features and patterns. The model then uses this knowledge to detect faces in new images, providing applications in various fields like security, surveillance, and human-computer interaction.

Taxi Sharing App

Taxi sharing apps, also known as ride-hailing services, have revolutionized the transportation industry. These apps connect passengers with nearby drivers using a smartphone platform. By optimizing routes and matching riders with available drivers, taxi sharing apps offer efficient and affordable transportation options. They have gained immense popularity due to their convenience, cost-effectiveness, and accessibility.
